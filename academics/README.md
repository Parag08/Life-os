## Study Principles

1. **Read before class** — even 30 min of skim is better than zero
2. **Take notes in your own words** — avoid copy-paste from slides
3. **Weekly consolidation** — spend 1 hour Sunday linking concepts
4. **Exam prep starts 3 weeks out** — no cramming
